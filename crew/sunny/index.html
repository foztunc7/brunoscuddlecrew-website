Sunny 🐤

The sunshine duck

Sunny is a little yellow duck in little red rain boots, and she has never met a puddle she didn't love. Everywhere she waddles, she splashes sunshine and happy quacks for everyone to share.

When the park gets dark or a corner looks spooky, Sunny lifts her glowing lantern high. No dark corner stays scary with Sunny around. She also looks after ten very wander-y little ducklings!

Things to know about Sunny

  • 🎨 Favorite color: Yellow, like the morning sun
  • 💦 Best at: puddle splashing and cheering friends up
  • 👢 Look for: red rain boots and a glowing lantern
  • 🐤 Family: ten little ducklings who love to wander off
"A yellow duck in rain boots, splashing sunshine everywhere with happy quacks for all to share!"
