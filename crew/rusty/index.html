Rusty 🦊

The fox with a plan

Rusty crosses his arms, huffs a little huff, and wears the grumpiest face in the whole park. But the moment somebody needs a hand, Rusty is the very first to make a plan.

Step one, step two, step three: Rusty thinks in lists, draws maps in the dirt, and never leaves a friend puzzled. And when the plan works? Biggest, hardest hug you ever got.

Things to know about Rusty

  • 🎨 Favorite color: Orange, like autumn leaves
  • 🗺️ Best at: making plans and drawing maps
  • 😤 Look for: crossed arms and a tiny huff
  • 🤗 Secret: he hugs the hardest of the whole crew
