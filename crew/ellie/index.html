Ellie 🐘

The gentle blue elephant

Ellie is big and blue and calm all the way through. She wears a soft green scarf, walks nice and slow, and listens with those great big ears better than anyone in the park.

When the day gets too loud or feelings get too big, Ellie knows just what to do. Breathe in... breathe out... and peace begins. The whole crew learned it from her.
