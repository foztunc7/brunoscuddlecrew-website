Lulu 🐰

The clever purple bunny

Lulu keeps one ear up and one ear down, so she never misses a thing. A duckling behind a lily pad? A sock under the bed? Lulu spots it first.

Sometimes her hops get wobbly and sometimes there are tears, but Lulu always hops up again. That's her secret: being brave starts small.

Things to know about Lulu

  • 🎨 Favorite color: Purple, like a lavender field
  • 👀 Best at: spotting things nobody else sees
  • 👂 Look for: one ear up, one ear down
  • 🥕 Favorite snack: carrot muffins, obviously
