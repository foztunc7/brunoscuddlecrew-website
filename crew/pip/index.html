Pip 🐶

The zoomiest puppy in the park

Pip is a quick little green puppy with a rescue helmet that never quite fits: it's always slipping over one eye. That never slows him down. When the Cuddle Crew heads out, Pip is first through the door, every single time.

He once chased a butterfly so far he got lost, and he learned the most important rescue rule of all: if you're ever lost, stay right there and call away!

Things to know about Pip

  • 🎨 Favorite color: Green, just like his fur
  • ⚡ Best at: zooming, sniffing out the way, wagging
  • ⛑️ Look for: the wobbly helmet over one eye
  • 🦋 Weakness: butterflies. He simply must follow them.
