Bruno 🐻

The big brave bear

Bruno is the leader of the Cuddle Crew, and the softest one too. He wears a cozy red hoodie with a golden paw-print badge, and when anyone in the park needs help, Bruno's paw is the first one up.

He's big, but never scary. When you're frightened, Bruno holds your paw until the scary feeling gets small enough to giggle at.

Things to know about Bruno

  • 🎨 Favorite color: Red, just like his hoodie
  • 💪 Best at: big warm hugs and being brave first
  • 🐾 Look for: the golden paw-print badge on his chest
  • 🍯 Favorite snack: berry honey toast
"He's a big brave bear, red as a berry, and when you're scared he holds your paw!"
